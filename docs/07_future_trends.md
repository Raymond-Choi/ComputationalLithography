# 07. Challenges and Future Trends / 挑战与未来趋势

## English
Computational lithography continues to evolve alongside EUV and high-NA systems.  
Future research focuses on:
- Multi-physics process modeling  
- Fast surrogate models and AI accelerators  
- Integration with digital twins and process control  
- Data-driven mask synthesis for yield improvement  

## 中文
随着 EUV 与高数值孔径光刻机的发展，计算光刻也面临新挑战与机遇。

**未来方向**
- 多物理场联合建模  
- 基于人工智能的快速近似模型  
- 与数字孪生及工艺控制的融合  
- 基于数据驱动的掩膜优化与良率提升
